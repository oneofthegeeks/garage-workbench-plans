# Garage Workbench Wall — Design Spec & Cut List

**Date:** 2026-03-12
**Location:** Garage, one wall
**Total Width:** 9'-0" (108")
**Work Surface Height:** 36"
**Cabinet Depth:** 30"
**Toe Kick Height:** 4"
**Build Approach:** 5 individual boxes, screwed together, continuous top

---

## Overview

A full-wall workbench and storage system consisting of five independent base cabinet boxes joined side-by-side along a 9-foot wall. A continuous 1.5" double-layer plywood workbench top spans the full length. A Ryobi miter saw is recessed into the center section at a surface 3" lower than the main work surface so the saw table sits flush with the bench top.

```
[ S1 · 24" ][ S2 · 20" ][ S3 · 20" ][ S4 · 20" ][ S5 · 24" ]
  3 drawers    shelves    miter saw    shelves     3 drawers
                         (3" lower)
|←————————————————— 9'-0" (108") ——————————————————→|
```

Each layer requires two pieces joined end-to-end (96" + 12" = 108"):

## Assembly Order

1. **Build toe kick bases** for all 5 sections. Level and fasten to floor and/or wall.
2. **Build cabinet boxes** (sides, top, bottom, back). No face frames — frameless construction.
3. **Set boxes on toe kick bases.** Screw adjacent boxes together through side panels.
4. **Install drawer slides** in S1 and S5. Build and fit drawer boxes.
5. **Install shelves** in S2 and S4.
6. **Laminate workbench top** (glue two layers, clamp overnight).
7. **Attach top** to cabinet boxes from below with 2" screws through the cabinet top panels.
8. **Attach drawer false fronts** to drawer boxes. Use temporary spacers to set the 1/8" reveal.
9. **Attach miter saw** — set Ryobi saw on the platform in S3 and confirm the saw table is flush with the 36" bench surface. Shim if needed.
10. **Fasten assembly to wall** — drive 3" screws through cabinet backs or a ledger rail into wall studs.

## Notes

- **MDF and moisture:** MDF will swell if it gets wet. Seal all exposed edges and surfaces with paint, shellac, or oil before use in the garage environment. The toe kick bases use plywood for this reason.
- **⚠️ MEASURE BEFORE CUTTING S3:** The Ryobi miter saw table height above its base determines the S3 box height. The spec assumes 3" (so S3 box = 27.5"). Measure your actual saw before cutting. Formula: S3 box height = 30.5" − (your saw's table height). If the table sits higher than 3", the box must be shorter. Cutting S3 too short cannot be undone.
- **Top seam placement:** Offset the seam in layer 1 and layer 2 of the workbench top so joints don't align. Stagger by at least 12".
- **Drawer slide type:** Full-extension, ball-bearing slides are strongly recommended for a shop environment (Blum, Accuride, or similar). Avoid cheap slides — they fail under heavy tool loads.
